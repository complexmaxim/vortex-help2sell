Welcome to akYtec

Welcome to the official website of akYtec - developer and manufacturer of various devices for measurement, control and regulation; as well as for different industrial sectors, agriculture or HVAC systems.

On our website you can find automation control products for both field and control level.

Control level

For the control level, we offer fully-fledged industrial PLCs with an integrated touchscreen. These intelligent devices are programmed in the worldwide known CODESYS 3.5 development environment. Each of these PLCs features a range of interfaces including RS485 and Ethernet, thereby allowing them to connect to any interface-relevant I/O units, for example, our digital or analog modules of the Mx110 series.

For simple automation tasks, our smart relays have proven their worth. They are significantly more affordable than conventional programmable logic controllers and are delivered with an intuitive programming software available at no charge.

In our product catalog, you can also find many process controllers for different physical quantities, such as temperature, pressure, humidity, etc. Our control technology is characterized by its reliability and ease of operation.

To capture and collect your process data, we offer a DIN-rail-mount data logger that can support up to 64 channels, operate in a Modbus network, and store data on an SD card.

Field level

In order to handle various industrial automation tasks at the field level more easily, we offer different transmitters, e.g. for:

  • - Pressure (gauge and vacuum), with different types of process connections and a wide variety of measuring ranges
  • - Temperature, with support for a wide range of various thermocouples or RTDs
  • - Humidity & temperature, with two analog outputs that can be configured as a 4-20 mA or 0-10 V output
  • - Gas (carbon dioxide or ammonia), available in different housing designs and an RS485 interface (Modbus protocol).

In order to visualize the required sensor data on the field level, we offer a wide range of digital displays characterized by their compact design. The displays can be mounted in a standard Ø22.5 mm mounting cutout.

Nowadays, the selection of reliable and cost-effective devices can be a considerable challenge for many industrial applications. Thanks to our reliable and high-quality control and regulation technology, your automated process control systems will successfully meet the fundamental objectives of optimizing operating procedures and have an excellent price-performance ratio.
